Process

Vision to Value

  • Curating Exceptional Projects

    We identify and originate high potential media and entertainment projects through a selective and strategic sourcing model.

    Our in-house teams work directly with creators, IP owners, and production entities to shape content aligned with global market demand.

  • Structuring for Success

    Each project is structured with scalability and international appeal in mind. We combine creative, commercial, and strategic inputs to build solid foundations, ready for capital allocation and market execution.

  • Accessing Capital via Our Investment Partnerships

    While 1808 is not a regulated entity and does not directly offer investment services, we collaborate with regulated investment firms who manage capital allocation into qualified projects. These firms independently assess, structure, and fund select opportunities.

  • Oversight & Execution

    We provide production oversight and strategic support throughout each project's lifecycle—helping to ensure that timelines, budgets, and creative goals are met, and that risk is actively managed.

  • Market Release & Revenue Generation

    Once completed, projects are brought to market via our network of distribution and platform partnerships. Our model prioritises global reach, audience engagement, and multi-format monetisation.

  • Performance Monitoring & Learnings

    We maintain a proprietary data loop across all our projects, analysing outcomes, forecasting trends, and refining our pipeline.

    This helps ensure consistent growth and forward-thinking reinvestment strategies.
